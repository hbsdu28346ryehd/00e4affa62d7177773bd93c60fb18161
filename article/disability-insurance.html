Disability Insurance

    Disability insurance is a type of insurance policy that provides financial benefits to individuals who are unable to work due to a disability. The policyholder receives a portion of their income in the form of monthly payments if they become disabled and cannot work for an extended period.

    Disability insurance can be provided through an employer or purchased independently. It is important to understand the terms and conditions of your policy, including the length of the waiting period before benefits are paid and the amount of coverage provided. Many policies also have exclusions and limitations that may impact your ability to receive benefits.

     Overall, disability insurance can provide financial security in the event of a disability that prevents you from working and earning an income. It is important to consider disability insurance as part of your overall financial plan and to consult with a financial advisor or insurance professional to determine the best policy for your needs.
