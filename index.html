What is Insurance?

Insurance is like a good friend who's always got your back when things go wrong. You pay a little bit of money upfront, and in return, they promise to help you out if something bad happens. It's like having a safety net for life's unexpected curveballs. Just remember, if your house burns down, your car gets stolen, or you get sick, don't forget to call your friend (aka your insurance company).
